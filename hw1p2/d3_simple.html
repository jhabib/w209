Interactive Chart using d3

This chart shows the number of hours I "sleep" at night, and the number of cups of coffee I drink the next morning. Hours of "sleep", and cups of coffee are plotted on the y-axis, and the date is plotted on the x-axis. The red line represents the hours of "sleep" and the blue line represents the cups of coffee. You can see that the less "sleep" I have at night, the more coffee I drink in the morning. The more coffee I drink in the morning, the less I "sleep" the next night. It's a vicious circle.

The blue dots show the hours of sleep, and the red dots show the cups of coffee.
Blue dotted line shows average of sleep, and red dotted line shows average of coffee.

Interactivity:

This graph provides two kinds of interactivity.

1. Select a day from the dropdown menu to filter the data by day of week. Select "All" to reset.
2. Hover mouse over the blue dots to increase size by number of hours. Hover mouse over the red dots to increase size by cups of coffee.
